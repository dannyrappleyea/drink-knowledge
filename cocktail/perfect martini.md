# Ingredients
* 1 1/2 oz gin
* 3/4 oz extra dry vermouth
* 3/4 oz sweet vermouth
* dash orange bitters


# Directions
* Stir all ingredients with ice and strain into chilled glass
* Garnish with luxardo cherry
