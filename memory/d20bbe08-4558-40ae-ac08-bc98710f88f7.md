# *Cocktails* Tokyo Daiquiri

## Ingredients
* 1/2 oz Fiery Ginger Syrup (Liber & Co)
* 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
* 2 oz aged rum
* 1/2-3/4 tsp low-sodium light soy sauce

## Directions
* Shake with ice, strain.

## Notes
* Makes a gingery drink, less syrup if that's too strong.
