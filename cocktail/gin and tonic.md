# Intro
Great summer sipping dring

# Ingredients
- 1.5 gin
- 4 oz tonic water
- lime wedge

# Directions
- Fill glass mostly full of ice. Squeeze lime and add. Pour gin and tonic. Can stir very lightly.

# Notes
- like navy strength gin
