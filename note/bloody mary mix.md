# Ingredients
* 6 oz can tomato paste
* 24 oz water (4 cans)
* 3 tbsp lime or lemon juice
* 3/4 tsp celery seed
* 1 tsp salt
* 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
* 2 tbsp prepared horseradish
* 1 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 tsp ghost pepper powder blend
* 1/2 tsp minced garlic

# Directions
1. Mix in Vitamix or blender
2. Chill
