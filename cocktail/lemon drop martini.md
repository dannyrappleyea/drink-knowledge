# Intro
Tasty, tart, ice-cold drink.

# Ingredients
* 1.5 oz vodka (or lemon vokda)
* 0.5 oz Cointreau
* 0.5 oz lemon juice
* 1 tsp maple syrup

# Directions
Mix all with ice and strain.

# Notes
* Add more maple syrup for a sweeter drink
